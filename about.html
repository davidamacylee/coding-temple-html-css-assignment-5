About

Established in 1978, Portland Tai Chi Academy proudly provides martial arts instructions in a variety of disciplines. Sifu Jeff Patterson is a student of Grand Master Sam Tam and carries the Yang Style Tai Chi lineage from China to Portland, Oregon. Portland Tai Chi Academy has seen more than 25,000 students since it first opened its doors.
